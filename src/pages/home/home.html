Pharmacy Medicine List

The purpose of this app is to inform the Clinicians of what is routinely stocked by the Avenues Clinic Pharmacy Department. When stocks are running low, the Department will inform the clinical areas as the initial communication. When stocks have run out where we cannot source stock from our wholesalers as they will also be out of stock, we will also communicate via an internal Memo.
